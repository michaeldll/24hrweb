About the game:

Counter Strike Global Offensive is a game pitting two teams agaisnt each other: Terrorists (T) and Counter-Terrorists (CT). Several scenarios are proposed in different game modes.

Game modes :

  • Defuse maps (whose name begins withde_): These are maps in which a bomb is planted by the Terrorist side on one of two sites. They win by detonating the bomb or killing all the CT. CT, meanwhile, win by killing all the terrorists or by defusing the bomb.
  • Counter-Strike maps (whose name begins with cs_): On these maps terrorists must keep the hostages and kill all CT, while CT have to rescue the hostages to take them to the extraction point, or kill all the terrorists.

Game modes :

  • Casual mode: Both teams compete to be the first to win in a best of 16 rounds. Here, when you die, you can not play before the next round. The teams consist of 2-16 players depending on the server where you play and the number of people connected. You earn money for each kill, when planting or defusing a bomb, rescuing a hostage and at the end of each round.
  • Competitive mode: it is similar to the casual mode except on two points: the two teams consist of 5 players and victories give you points. These results give you a rank, which varies depending on your progress and skill.
  • "Deathmatch" mode: here, the two camps are fighting over a map. With no money management, you choose your weapon freely. With each death, you come back into play instantly.
  • "Demolition" mode: you play on maps specifically designed for this mode. At each round, the game assigns you random weapons if you've made a kill in the previous round. The victory is given in a best of 20 rounds.
  • "Gun Game": this game mode is played on specific maps. The two teams clash but the game is more personal. As soon as you kill someone, you change your weapon ... The goal is to be the first to achieve a kill with the golden knife.
